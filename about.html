our beginning

  • We introduced Trust Art at TED in 2009, issuing stock certificates to people making gifts to public artists. We started a community of shareholders backing ongoing projects.
  • We find inspiration in the story of the Statue of Liberty, which had as much art and imagination in the funding and community-building efforts as in the sculpture itself.
  • The initiative created the kind of iconic art that renews society's ideas of what is possible.

HOW it works

  • You may gift monies, materials, or time over this website to any project.
  • You are given one share for every dollar you give.
  • Shares may in turn be given to your friends and acquaintances, passing the gift along to a larger community.
  • Every three years, the ART from completed projects is auctioned, and any profits are shared between artists and shareholders.
  • The First Trust Art Triennial Auction is Feb 14, 2012.

Together they founded Fame Theory LLC, an online media platform profiling people and events in the arts, business, philanthropy and New York society.
